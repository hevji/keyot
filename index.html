// what it does

01

YouTube, Spotify & more

yt-dlp under the hood means 1000+ sites work out of the box. TikTok, Instagram, Twitter, Vimeo, SoundCloud â€” if yt-dlp supports it, yoink does too.

02

MP4, MP3 or FLAC

best quality video by default. --audio for MP3 at 192kbps. --flac for lossless. yoink figures out the rest.

03

no ads. ever.

completely open source. no sketchy websites with 4 popups. no installers that bundle toolbars. just a command that works.

04

works everywhere

installer puts yoink on your PATH. open any terminal â€” cmd, powershell, windows terminal â€” and just type yoink.

05

smart defaults

set a default folder once, never get asked again. spotify and audio downloads auto-route to ~/Music. playlists ask before you download 200 videos.

06

--pick flag

not sure yoink found the right version? --pick shows top 5 YouTube results with durations so you choose exactly what you want.
